To the One I Love

I know there must be lakhs of people like me who send messages to people like you. But I only have you with me.

I know that if you want, you can choose not to talk to someone like me — and it probably won’t make any difference to you.

But if you ever have a night where you can’t sleep… ask me how it feels, because I stay awake every night thinking about you 🌙💭.

I'm sorry to say this, but maybe you don’t have any feelings for me. Still, I have unlimited feelings for you — endless, unshakable, eternal 💞♾️.

The moon may stop rising… the sun may stop shining… But my love for you will never fade, not even for a second ☀️🌕❤️.

Maybe you think I talk big, but trust me — my feelings are even bigger than my words. I love you a thousand times more than I could ever express 💖.

I don’t know what your past holds… But I promise, your future will be beautiful — and I’m not saying this just to say it. If needed, I can write all of this for you — even on stamp paper — because that’s how serious I am 📝🖋️.

Please try to understand me: If you’re with me, everything feels right ✨. If you’re not… everything feels meaningless 😔.

When you're around, I feel alive 😊. Without you, I lose interest in everything 🥀.

Not everything can be explained in a single message, but I know you'll try to understand. And I promise you — you’ll never be hurt by my heart 💓🙏.

I know I still have to prove a lot to you… But what I do know is: you matter to me more than anything else in the world 🌍❤️.

People often have a reason for liking someone. But I don’t have a specific reason — I just feel it deeply… without explanation 🌌.

There’s one thing I want to say — I have liked you since the day I first met you 🌸. I just didn’t have the courage to say it earlier. Now that I’ve got this chance, I don’t want to let you go 🤝.

I can wait a lifetime for you ⏳. But I could never wait for someone else the way I wait for you 💘.

I wish I had been your first love… I wish I could be the one who never breaks your heart… And I wish — from the bottom of my heart — that one day, you feel the same for me ❤️‍🔥.

I know I may not be your first love, But believe me — I will love you so deeply, That neither you’ve felt a love like this before, Nor will you ever feel it again ❣️.

Even in silence, my heart speaks only your name.
